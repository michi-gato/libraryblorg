That one time I thought I could be the neighborhood baker.

Moving from California Bay Area to New York City left me in sourdough drought. I missed the thick crunchy toasted outer layer, and then biting into the fluffy tangy soury bready innards. This city of world-reknowned restauranteurs had flour water compared to the yeasty loaves in the Bay. And thus, Taaffe Loaffes was born. This 24 hour-rise no-knead bread made me better human being.
